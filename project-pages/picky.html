Picky

Food ordering app concept

Overview

I created a food ordering app for people with unique diets. From initial user research and sketching wireframes to crafting a high fidelity prototype, I took on the challenge of simplifying restaurant choices for those dealing with allergies, health goals, or specific preferences. The app smooths out dietary filtering and offers an easy way to compare restaurant offerings, all aimed at giving your dining experience a boost. 
The project was completed as part of the Google UX Design Certificate program.
