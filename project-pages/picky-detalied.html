The food ordering app to find the food to fit your diet

Overview

my role

Timeline

August 2022 – November 2022

Project Type

UX Design

Tools

Figma, Maze, Adobe Illustrator, Miro

The problem

More and more people follow special diets, be it for food allergies and insensitivities, health goals, religion or personal preferences. It is challenging and time consuming to select restaurants with a compatible offering, which discourages exploring new places.

The goal

Design a food ordering app that allows for the quick filtering based on a person’s special dietary needs and offers a tool for the intuitive comparison of the compatibility of each restaurants offering.
